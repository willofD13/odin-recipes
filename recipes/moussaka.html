Moussaka

Description

Making moussaka comes down to preparing the eggplants, potatoes, beef tomato sauce and thd the bechamel sauce. Finally you should assembly the ingredients and bake them until perfectly golden brown!

Ingredients

  • eggplants
  • potatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • beef
  • bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes in uniform disks.
  2. Rinse them under some running water to get rid of the excess starch.
  3. Slice the eggplants in about the same thickness as the potatoes.
  4. Rinse them under the water until it comes out clear.
  5. Drizzle the eggplants and potatoes with some olive oil and season them with a good pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Bake them in sheet pans in 180 C for 20 minutes.
  7. Saute the ground meat in olive oil, onions and garlic.
  8. Deglaze with some red wine.
  9. Simmer with chopped tomatoes until thickened.
  10. Prepare your bechamel sauce. Start my melting the butter.
  11. Add all of the flour, whisk it until the butter is fully absorbed into the flour and let it cook for a couple of minutes until it slightly browns.
  12. Turn down the heat to medium and add the milk a little bit at a time. Wait for the milk to absorb into the flour, add some more and repeat until youâ€™ve used all the milk in the recipe. Your bechamel sauce is ready when it has thickened and resembles a custard.
  13. Remove the pan from the stove and add the cheese, the nutmeg and two teaspoons of salt.
  14. Whisk until combined, taste your bechamel and add one more teaspoon of salt if it still tastes bland.
  15. Finally add your egg yolks and quickly whisk so the eggs fully incorporate in the mixture.
